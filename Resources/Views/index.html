ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

RPS is a peer to peer iOS game. Designed to get friends in touch by playing the famous hand game .

Most Loved Features

  • No sign up needed
  • Discover people around you
  • Play RPS
  • Group Chat

Free Download. No Sign Up Required

How does it work?

You have two options to connect and play with your friends.

    1 - Sending Notifications
  • Select Discover Friends Around You
  • Send an invite to a friend
  • Wait your friend to send you his/her acceptance
  • Accept then Play
    2 - Manual Selection
  • Open Board
  • Select your friend as opponent
  • Ask your friend to select you as opponent
  • Start Play
